About Me

I’m an independent researcher in theoretical physics focusing on modified gravity and cosmology. My work aims to reinterpret gravity as a scalar pressure phenomenon, unifying classical and quantum structure without invoking dark matter.

Current Research

Title: A Universal Framework for the Pressure Field Theory of Gravity (PFTG-MinimalRelic)

Summary: This framework proposes a scalar field Φ that produces gravitational effects via pressure gradients, eliminating the need for dark matter. The theory recovers GR results in weak fields, aligns with cosmological observations (CMB, M87*), and predicts testable deviations in strong-field regimes.
