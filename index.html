Overview

The Pressure-Field Theory of Gravity (PFTG-MinimalRelic) replaces spacetime curvature with vacuum energy pressure gradients. It matches GR in weak fields and removes the need for dark matter while introducing quantization, entropy, and testable deviations.

  • Scalar Lagrangian with photon/entropy terms
  • Orbit drift, lensing, CMB match, black hole edges
  • 12 validation simulations with visual appendix
  • Quantized emergence of ℏ, birefringence, inflation
