🪐 What is PFTG?

The Pressure-Field Theory of Gravity (PFTG–MinimalRelic) explains gravity as pressure from scalar field gradients — not spacetime curvature. It removes the need for dark matter and unifies general relativity with quantum effects using a single scalar field (Φ).
